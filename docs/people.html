Principal Partner

CHEW YONG SEE 丘容僖

Mr. Chew Yong See is a distinguished legal practitioner with a prestigious academic background and extensive experience in corporate law and finance. Graduating from the esteemed University of Melbourne, Australia, in 1994, he earned dual degrees in Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting) and Bachelor of Laws. In January 1996, Mr. Chew was called to the High Court of Malaya, Malaysia, as an "Advocate and Solicitor," marking the beginning of a distinguished legal career.

Mr. Chew commenced his practice with Messrs Rashid & Lee, Kuala Lumpur, one of Malaysia's largest corporate and commercial legal firms at the time. During his tenure, he specialized in a wide array of corporate matters, including capital market financing, corporate banking, venture capital investments, joint ventures, and corporate advisory work. In August 2002, Mr. Chew transitioned to Messrs Ghazi & Lim, Kuala Lumpur, where he co-led the corporate and finance department until October 2007.

In addition to his corporate practice, Mr. Chew has made significant contributions to the sports and telecommunications sectors in Malaysia. He has been appointed as an independent advisor to the National Sports Council of Malaysia, the office of the Sports Commissioner of Malaysia, and the Malaysia Communications and Multimedia Commission, demonstrating his expertise in regulatory matters and legislative initiatives

With his wealth of experience, impeccable reputation, and commitment to excellence, Mr. Chew Yong See is recognized as a leading legal professional in Malaysia's corporate landscape.
